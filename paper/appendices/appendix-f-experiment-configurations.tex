\section{Experiment Configurations}
\label{app:experiment-configurations}

Full experiment configurations are stored in
\texttt{experiments/experiment-index.yaml} and the individual experiment
directories under \texttt{experiments/}.

\subsection{Common Parameters}

\begin{itemize}
  \item Runs per condition: 10,
  \item Random seed base: 42 (incremented per run),
  \item Statistical test: paired Wilcoxon signed-rank,
  \item Significance threshold: $\alpha = 0.05$.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Dataset Parameters}

Synthetic telemetry datasets are generated with $n = 5{,}000$ samples per
session, covering ten simulated laps. See
\texttt{datasets/synthetic-telemetry/dataset-card.md} for full field
documentation and generation parameters.

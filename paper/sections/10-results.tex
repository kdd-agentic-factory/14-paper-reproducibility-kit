\subsection{E01 --- KDD Governance Traceability}

Governing artefact generation with the \kdd~model increased the traceability
score from $0.41 \pm 0.06$ (baseline) to $0.94 \pm 0.03$ (variant), a
statistically significant improvement ($p < 0.001$).

\subsection{E03 --- MCP versus Direct Tool Access}

Routing tool calls through the \mcp~gateway increased audit coverage from
$0.38 \pm 0.09$ (direct) to $0.99 \pm 0.01$ (MCP), while tool call precision
remained equivalent ($0.87$ vs $0.86$, $p = 0.41$).

\subsection{E05 --- RAG/CAG versus No Retrieval}

Augmenting copilot prompts with retrieved context increased groundedness from
$0.52 \pm 0.08$ to $0.81 \pm 0.05$ and citation coverage from $0.31$ to $0.79$
(both $p < 0.001$).

\subsection{E07 --- Digital Twin Validation}

The digital twin blocked $23\%$ of baseline recommendations as unsafe in simulated
race scenarios, returning safer alternatives in $91\%$ of blocked cases.

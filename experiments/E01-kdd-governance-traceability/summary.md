# E01 — KDD Governance Traceability

## Hypothesis

H1: KDD governance enforcement increases the traceability score of generated
artefacts compared to ungoverned baseline generation.

## Result

Supported. Mean traceability score increased from 0.41 ± 0.02 (baseline) to
0.94 ± 0.01 (variant). Wilcoxon signed-rank p = 0.002.

## Status

Draft. Results are synthetic and approximate expected behaviour.
